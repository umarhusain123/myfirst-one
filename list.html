Umar's recipe for the best chicken rolls

Ingredients

for the dough:

  • 3/4 cup warm milk
  • teaspoon yeast
  • masoor dal
  • moong daal

for the fillings:

  • oil
  • chawal
  • chicken
  • birayani masala

instructions:

  1. first take the bowl and take water in hot and start boiling it
  2. then add some rice in that hot water and steam the rice
  3. add masala which we prepared before and stir
